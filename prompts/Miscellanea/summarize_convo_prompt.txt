summary that can help the  agent to continue with the conversation without loosing context.

1. names, dates and places mentioned in the conversation
2. Important facts that could allow a gpt agent to answer correctly to new messages from the user

(summary "actual text of the summary" "last message from the user" "last reply from the agent")
